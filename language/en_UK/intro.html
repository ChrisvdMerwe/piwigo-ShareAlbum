Share Album plugin enables you to share albums through a simple click.
For each shared album, it generates for a unique URL enabling autologin using a (unique) user, then having only access to the shared album.
Share menu is available to administrator users in each Private category page

Important : The share link will only be displayed when albums are not publicly shared (those one do not need a specific plugin for being shared).
If you can not see the share icon, please verify that album is hidden from public. Please also check that your theme is supported by the plugin.
